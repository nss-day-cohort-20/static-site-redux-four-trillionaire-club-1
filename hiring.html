Work for Winions Enterprises

Winions Enterprises is always looking for capable people. If you have experience in training people in hand-to-hand combat, mindless obedience, pointless self-sacrifice. Also, if you you have a background in genetic engineering, psychological programming and de-programming, or if you just like to vent about old stories of past minion incompetence; we'd like to talk to you.

Positions at Winions Enterprises

    Our Training Instructors derive great joy from turning our new Winions from worthless pathetic lumps of incompetence into shining examples of evil wish fulfillment.

    Our psychological programmers are skilled in shaping the minds of our Winions. They can set up courses of treatment to instill the mindless obedience needed from our recruits as well as re-program the minds of those that are captured.
