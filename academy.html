Winion Training - Our Philosophy

Our winions are subjected to a brutal, uncompromising standards in order to ensure the success of your master plan. As evil overlords ourselves we've endured the humilation of defeat by the damnable heroes countless times because of our weak-minded minions. A solution for this critical, frustrating, disappointing, utterly unacceptable and consistant failure point needed to be found thus we designed our training to eliminate such weaknesses.

Combat Training!

Marksmenship.

In order to provide the proper motivation to hit their targets minions who fail their marksmanship training are used as target practice for the next class.

Attacking all at once.

What is the point of acquiring numerous followers if they cannot use the common sense to overwhelm heroes? Instead of lining up to die like it is some kind of movie, our Winions cooperate to overwhelm heroes in team-building classes taught by our staff.

Dealing with plot armor.

One of the most important classes taught by our instructors, Dealing With Plot Armor, is critical to subjugating or destroying the world. Our Winions can correctly identify the weaknesses of heroes and actually hit them!

Winionception App

If you are not winning as much as you require, our Winionception app will allow you to upgrade your Winions to even more powerful versions! Simply download and request the upgrade packages to be delivered to the specified location. Even in the midst of climactic battle you can turn the tide with a well timed upgrade!
